About Me

I am an aspiring data scientist with a deep appreciation for inter and transdisciplinarity. My main focus is human and development geography, which allows me to explore how people interact with and use their environment, and the reasoning and logics behind these interactions. While development could be considered my “special” interest, I’ve always been curious about every industry, including mining, construction, finance, and agriculture, because I love understanding how different systems work and connect. Curiosity drives me, and I believe in embracing embarrassment and failure as part of the learning process. After all, if we don’t try and fail, how can our brains learn and train themselves to improve? This philosophy aligns with the words of Will Durant:

Excellence is never an accident. It is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, and intelligent execution; it represents the wise choice of many alternatives - choice, not chance, determines your destiny.

From The Story of Philosophy (2026)
